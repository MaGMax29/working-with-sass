Some of my work and inspiration

Street Photography

Street Photography has always been one of my passions. I enjoy going out and taking pictures that reflects the reality of our world. I always find new stories to tell, new places to photograph and the excitement of capturing a moment in life.

Illustrations

I started using watercolours and ink recently, falling in love with these medium. I feel more comfortable using graphite for my drawings but I am always willing to experiment with other techniques. It makes me happy when I try something new, especially when art is involved.

Textile Art

Handmade creations have a special place in my heart. I love to create textile pieces that represents my personal taste and interests while expressing real meaning. There is something quite significant when something is made by your own hands.
